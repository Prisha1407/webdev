Bluetooth Controlled RC Car

This Bluetooth RC car operates by integrating Arduino, Motors, and Bluetooth module. The Arduino serves as the brain of the car, interpreting signals received via Bluetooth. The Bluetooth module facilitates wireless communication with a paired device, mobile phone in this case. The car can be controlled through an existing smartphone application and through serial monitor too that sends commands, such as forward, backward, left, and right to the Arduino.
